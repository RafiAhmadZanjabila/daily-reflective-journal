Mood Tracking

Keeping track of your mood on a daily basis can help you better understand your emotional patterns and wellbeing. With the mood tracking feature, you can log how you feel each day, select from a range of moods, and see how your emotions evolve over time. The visual mood history allows you to identify trends, whether you're riding a wave of positivity or managing periods of stress. This insight into your emotional state can serve as a valuable tool for personal growth and self-awareness.

Diary Entries

Express your thoughts, feelings and experiences with the diary entry feature. Writing daily reflections allows you to capture the essence of your day and track your mental and emotional journey. You can organize your entries by date, reflect on past moments and gain deeper insights into your personal growth. Whether you're documenting major life events or small daily details, this space becomes your personal journal - a safe haven for self-expression and introspection.

Habit Tracking

Building better habits is key to improving your lifestyle and the habit tracking feature helps you stay on top of your goals. Whether you're trying to exercise more or read daily, you can set habits to track over time. The tool provides a simple way to monitor progress and celebrate your victories. By keeping an eye on your routines, you'll see how your small daily actions lead to big improvements over time.
